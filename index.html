Soaps: clean and fresh, baby powder, goat’s milk and chamomile, honey and goat’s milk, and lavender.

Body Washes: citrus, fresh linen, wild currant and sandalwood, sea breeze, pear, and amber wood.

Hand Creams: chocolate, wild currant and sandalwood, honey suckle, jasmine, and chinese waterlilly.
